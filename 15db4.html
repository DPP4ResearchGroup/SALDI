In addition, some community developed MALDI-based MSI pipeline has been made available through Mass Spectrometry Imaging Society (maldi-msi.org). Nevertheless, due to the co-existing of independent data formats depend on MSI instrumentation vendors, the MSI data analysis is largely independent practice in the past.  The Recent adaptation of common imzML data format \citep{22842151} within major instrument vendors has allowed the development of the independent analysing tools and more vivid statistical analysis to identify analyte distributions and colocalizations more accurately.
